GENERAL PURPOSE

Under the direction of the General Manager, the Operations Manager is responsible for managing and delivering an efficient and effective logistics service through, planning, personnel supervision, fleet maintenance, customer satisfaction and cost reduction efforts.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

› Supervises work performed in the facility laundry area including receiving, pick up, sorting, cleaning and distribution of laundry to customers.

› Handle and troubleshoot all issues posed by supervisors and ensure proper resolution.

› Ensures appropriate housekeeping standards are met.

› Orders, receives and properly distributes all necessary supplies needed to ensure proper work flow.

› Assuring the cost-effective operation of the plant, while maintaining a safe work environment for the staff.

› Maintains daily vehicle maintenance logs and documentation of incident reports.

› Complies with all DOT rules and regulations, company policies and guidelines.

› Manage fleet equipment to ensure maximum efficiency and profitability.

› Ensures trucks are in proper working condition and preventative maintenance on trucks is performed on a timely basis

› Ensure that all drivers are in compliance with Safety requirements promoting the company’s safety culture, including training, clearly stating safety expectations, ensuring unsafe events are reviewed in a timely manner.

› Understand and satisfy the customer’s needs, goals, a degree of urgency and work as a professional business partner to achieve all goals.

› Creates and reviews procedures for distribution and inventory management to maximize customer satisfaction and minimize cost.

› Builds company image by collaborating with customers and enforcing ethical business practices

› Maintains quality service by enforcing organization delivery standards.

› Compiles data on employee productivity and utility usage in order to produce daily operational reports.

› Supervises the scheduling of routes and ensures deliveries are completed in a timely and accurate manner

› Supervises employees to include the initial training of the new hires prior to actual work performed.

› Responsible for Bins Inventory and Control to include controls of transit.

› Responsible for Scale Inventory at all customer locations.

› Responsible for documenting/addressing employee’s unsatisfactory work performance and/or discipline issues as needed to correct unsatisfactory behavior within company’s established guidelines.

› Responsible for collecting data and producing trend and planning reports.

› Available to work some weekends

› Willing to drive up to 15 miles

CORE COMPETENCIES

* Marketing Initiatives * Product Positioning and Marketing * Market Intelligence and Market Penetration * Process * Improvement and Customer Relationship Management * Competitive Analysis * Vendor Management * Cross-Selling Strategies * Operational Cost Reduction * Product Launches *

QUALIFICATIONS/SKILLS/EDUCATION

› Must have a clean driving record.

› Must have experience with scheduling routes.

› Spanish language skills a plus.

› Ability to communicate effectively in written format and oral presentations.

› Ability to multi-task and establish priorities.

› Ability to maintain organization in a changing environment

› Exhibits imitative, responsibility, flexibility, and leadership.

› Ability to use knowledge of the working environment to meet established goals and objectives.

› Proficient in the use of Windows based office software including but not limited to Microsoft Office, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook.

› Awareness of and strategic response to external influences, such as legislation, fuel costs, and environmental pressures.

› Will be required to perform other duties as requested, directed or assigned.

› Must have a flexible schedule, available to work Holidays and Weekends.
